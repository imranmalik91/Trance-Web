1. Box Breathing (4-4-4-4 Method)

Perfect for calming nerves in stressful moments.

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
  2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  3. Exhale slowly for 4 seconds.
  4. Hold again for 4 seconds.

Repeat for 5‚Äì10 minutes. Great for work stress, presentations, or anytime you feel overwhelmed.

2. 4-7-8 Breathing for Deep Relaxation

A powerful technique to release tension and prepare for sleep.

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
  2. Hold your breath for 7 seconds.
  3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 8 seconds.

Repeat 4 cycles. Ideal before bedtime or when anxiety feels high.

3. Diaphragmatic (Belly) Breathing

Trains your body to breathe fully and deeply.

  1. Sit or lie down comfortably.
  2. Place one hand on your chest, the other on your belly.
  3. Inhale slowly, letting your belly rise (not your chest).
  4. Exhale gently and feel your belly fall.

Continue for 10 minutes. Perfect for daily practice to strengthen calmness.
